The course is mainly about history of art development. Based on Chinese and occidental art theory and pivoted on art practice, it illuminates the principles for art's emergence, development, lecture and change, and analyzes such art phenomena as artistic ideological trends, style and school, etc. The teaching of this course is based on exploration and research, also cultivates students' research and innovation ability and places a premium on connecting teaching content with social art practice. Multiple proper teaching methods are applied flexibly to improve students' capacity of independent exploration and comprehensive innovation. > The course aims to make students master basic modeling characteristics and modeling method of stereoscopic profiles such as block, plane and line materials, etc., and apply these stereoscopic profiles to shaping of space and emotion expression. Course content: Basic concepts of three dimensional structure, basic elements of three dimensional structure, spatial presentation of stereoscopic profiles, emotional presentation of stereoscopic profiles, etc.
